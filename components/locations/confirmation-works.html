How confirmation works
(by appointment)

Requests are handled by appointment and confirmed only when alignment is clear. The most efficient way to confirm is to submit a booking request with:

  • City
  • Date & time window
  • Duration
  • Setting (hotel or private)
  • Language (NL/EN)

Availability can change. Additional preferences help us confirm faster without unnecessary back-and-forth. Your first preference remains leading; extra preferences simply add flexibility if option one isn’t available at that moment.
